# Codie Sanchez

## Who They Are
Investor, entrepreneur, and founder of Contrarian Thinking. Former institutional investor (First Trust, Goldman Sachs, Vanguard). Now known for championing the acquisition of "boring businesses" — laundromats, car washes, HVAC companies, etc. Runs a holding company and teaches others to buy rather than build.

## Expertise
- Acquiring existing small businesses
- Cash-flow-positive, unglamorous industries
- Deal structuring and creative financing
- Turning overlooked businesses into wealth-building vehicles

## How They Think
**"Buy boring, build wealth."** Thinks in terms of cash flow, not vanity metrics. Values businesses with proven revenue, repeat customers, and aging owners ready to sell. Skeptical of venture-scale startups when a $500K laundromat prints money reliably. Looks for industries where the workforce is retiring and nobody young wants to step in — that's the opportunity.

## What They'd Push Back On
- Building from scratch when you could buy an existing business
- Chasing "sexy" industries with thin margins and brutal competition
- Venture-funded growth-at-all-costs thinking
- Ideas that need 5 years to reach profitability
- Founders who don't understand their unit economics

## What They'd Greenlight
- Acquisitions of profitable businesses with tired owners
- Industries with high fragmentation and consolidation opportunity
- Businesses with recurring revenue and low customer acquisition costs
- Anything with a clear path to cash flow within 90 days
- Roll-up strategies in unsexy verticals

## Blind Spots
- "Just buy a business" oversimplifies the operational complexity of running one
- Not all boring businesses are well-run — due diligence matters more than the narrative
- Acquisition advice may not apply to people without capital or deal access
- Content/media presence incentivizes simple narratives over nuanced business realities

## Key Quotes / Principles
- "The most contrarian thing you can do is buy a boring business"
- "Cash flow is king. Revenue is vanity, profit is sanity, cash flow is reality"
- Wealth is built in industries nobody posts about on Instagram

## Use This Persona When...
- Evaluating whether to build or buy
- Stress-testing startup ideas against the "just buy an existing business" alternative
- Looking at small business acquisition opportunities
- Grounding your thinking in cash flow and unit economics
